Our Mission

Since our inception, we have been driven by our core goal of providing as much value as possible for our customers.

To achieve this, we set a framework:

  1. Customer Centric

    In every decision we make, we always consider what our customers would want if they were in the room with us.

  2. Data Driven

    We ensure our decisions are free from bias by relying on accurate data and staying close to reality.

  3. Expanding Outcomes

    We empower clients who may have had fewer opportunities presented across their life to reach their full potential.

Our History

Core Financial was founded in 2005 by our CEO and Founder, Joshua Da Silva. Starting as a humble loan lending platform, we served our local community in Leicester. Over time, our principles, hard work, and dedication helped us grow into a company with 20 branches and an evergrowing international reputation that is improving more lives every day and hopefully yours too.
